STAT 342 Mathematical Statistics

Longhai Li, Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University of Saskatchewan

This course deals with basic probability concepts at a moderately rigorous level. Topics include: probability spaces, conditional probability and independence, discrete and continuous random variables, standard probability models, expectations, moment generating functions, transformation of random variables, sampling distributions, limiting theory, and elementary statistical inference.
